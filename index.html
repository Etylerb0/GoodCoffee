LES's Hidden Gem?
We sure are!

Located in NYC's LES neighborhood Good Coffee Co. opened its nooks and benches to the locals here in 2015. Providing the finest of coffee beans from our international sources. Our beans create a stimulus so natural you'll appreciate the non-crash effect. Bring your favorite cardigan, tablet , or book and grab a hot cup of joy to relax with! Hope to see you soon! NOTE 5% of every purchase goes back into the coffee farms to improve pay and working conditions.

Sources

Guatemala, South Africa, Malawi, Kenya, Brasil, Costa Rica, and Argentina. Our Farmers are paid fairly and are able to provide jobs to others. Working conditions are improving with every sale, aiming to provide a lunch program.

More than Good Coffee.

We feature over 30 vegan pastries. Local farm produced fruit. We only use raw sugar for that sinful sweet your Grandmas cookies have. Grab a danish, muffin, or bagel with your coffee and enjoy your morning!
